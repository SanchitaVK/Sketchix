About Sketchix

Sketchix is a website which has a free comic readind and creating application that is open to the public and intended to be easily used by all ages. Sketchix was created to communicate ideas creatively through cartoons and as a way to offer artistic expression for those who do not have the talent to draw. The website lets users easily read,create and share cartoons. For ease of use, ComicWorld and our cartoon artists created a wide variety of characters, backdrops,props, text options, and other special features an idea for the cartoon is all that is needed.
